 📊 METRICS PROVIDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

MEETINGS:
├─ Total Meetings (all user bookings)
└─ Upcoming Meetings (future bookings)

MESSAGES:
├─ Total Messages (all conversations)
└─ Unread Messages (estimated ~15% of total)

CONNECTIONS:
└─ Total Connections (user's network size)

ENGAGEMENT SCORE:
├─ Formula: (Meetings × 10) + (Messages ÷ 3.33) + (Connections × 2) + Bonus
├─ Range: 0-100%
└─ Status: Getting started | Good | Excellent

ACTIVITY TIMELINE:
├─ 30-day history
├─ Meetings trend
├─ Messages trend
└─ Connections trend
